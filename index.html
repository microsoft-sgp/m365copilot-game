How to Play

Complete Copilot Chat tasks on your 3×3 board, verify your answers, and earn keywords when you complete a Bingo line.

  1. Pick a pack (1–999) and enter your name → Launch your board.
  2. Click any tile to open the task panel.
  3. Copy the prompt → Open Copilot Chat → Paste and run it.
  4. Paste your proof into the text box → Verify & Claim.
  5. Complete a row, column, or diagonal → earn a keyword!
  6. Submit keywords on the Submit tab to score for your org.
⚠️ Browser Security Note: Due to standard browser security restrictions, this game cannot automatically fill or send your prompt into Copilot Chat (m365.cloud.microsoft/chat) via URL parameters. The supported flow is: (1) Copy prompt to clipboard → (2) Open Copilot Chat → (3) Paste manually. This is by design.
🔒 Keyword Security Note (Prototype): Keywords are generated client-side using a session nonce for per-user uniqueness. This is a prototype and is not tamper-proof. In production, keywords should be generated and validated server-side (e.g., Azure Functions) against a registry, and client-side storage should not be trusted.
